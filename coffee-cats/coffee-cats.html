Coffee & Cats

The Rooms

We offer two private kitty rooms. Each is decorated with colorful details that will make the most discriminating Instagram follower take notice of your amazing pics! Come enjoy time with one our foster kitties...heck, you might even fall in love and what to adopt your new found friend!

The Kitties

We are careful to select our most friendly kitties for our Coffee & Cats experience. The kitties are all in our foster homes and come for a special visit to the Coffee & Cats Café! All of our kitties have been vaccinated and are ready to show you some fun and love. You never know what types of personalities you will find with our loving foster kitties. Heck, you might want to book both rooms for a visit! Sessions are 30 minutes long and accommodate up to three people per session. See our FAQs below for ages and restrictions.

Where is Coffee & Cats?

Coffee & Cats is a little kitty café inside of Cat's Cradle. We are located at 122 South Main Street in Harrisonburg, VA. We are open for Coffee & Cat Sessions on Saturday from 10am - 4:30pm. Your support of this program helps our Spay/Neuter Program!
